Why AI-Powered Validation?

According to CB Insights, 42% of startups fail because there is no market need. Traditional validation takes weeks of manual research. Test Your Idea compresses that research into a 60-second automated scan by cross-referencing live data sources that would take a human analyst days to cover.

The goal is not to replace founder judgment, but to surface the risks, competitors, and market signals that founders typically discover too late.

The 13-Dimension Analysis Framework

Every idea is evaluated across 13 independent dimensions. Each dimension contributes to the overall viability score.

  • 1 Overview — Market context, problem definition, target audience sizing
  • 2 Validation Signals — Evidence of real demand from search trends, forums, social media
  • 3 Competitor Landscape — Direct and indirect competitors, funding data, feature gaps
  • 4 SWOT Analysis — Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats mapped to market data
  • 5 Differentiation Strategy — Unique angles, underserved niches, positioning opportunities
  • 6 Revenue Model — Pricing benchmarks, monetization paths, unit economics estimates
  • 7 MVP Roadmap — Core features, build vs. buy decisions, launch timeline
  • 8 Go-to-Market Plan — Acquisition channels ranked by cost and fit
  • 9 Risk Assessment — Regulatory, technical, and market risks with mitigation strategies
  • 10 Key Assumptions — Critical hypotheses that must be true for the idea to work
  • 11 Community Signals — Reddit, Hacker News, IndieHackers sentiment and discussions
  • 12 Actionable Playbook — Week-by-week execution plan tailored to the idea
  • 13 Synthesis & Verdict — Final viability score with confidence intervals

Methodology Principles

Evidence over opinion

Every claim in the report is backed by a data source. If no evidence exists for a market signal, the report says so explicitly rather than speculating.

Transparent limitations

AI validation cannot predict founder execution quality, timing luck, or black swan events. The report is a research accelerator, not a verdict. We state confidence levels for each finding.

Actionable output

Reports include specific next steps, not vague advice. Each recommendation links to the evidence that supports it, so founders can verify and decide independently.

Continuous calibration

The scoring model is periodically recalibrated against real startup outcomes. As the dataset of scanned ideas grows (currently 2,500+), pattern recognition improves.
